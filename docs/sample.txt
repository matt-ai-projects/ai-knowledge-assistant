ACME Corp Product Manual

Chapter 1: Getting Started
The ACME X200 is a cloud-based project management tool designed for enterprise teams.
To get started, visit app.acme.com and create an account using your company email address.
Once logged in, you will be prompted to create your first workspace.

Chapter 2: User Roles
There are three user roles in ACME X200: Admin, Manager, and Viewer.
Admins can invite users, manage billing, and configure integrations.
Managers can create projects, assign tasks, and view reports.
Viewers can only read project data and cannot make changes.

Chapter 3: Integrations
ACME X200 integrates with Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Google Workspace.
To enable Slack notifications, go to Settings, then Integrations, then click Connect Slack.
All integration credentials are encrypted and stored securely.

Chapter 4: Pricing
ACME X200 offers three pricing tiers: Starter, Professional, and Enterprise.
The Starter plan is free for up to 5 users.
The Professional plan costs $12 per user per month and includes advanced reporting.
The Enterprise plan includes custom pricing, SSO, and dedicated support.
